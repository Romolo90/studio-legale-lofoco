Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains which cookies and similar technologies we use on our website and how you can manage them.

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that are saved on your device when you visit a website. They help improve navigation, remember your preferences, and in some cases collect statistical information about site usage.

2. Types of cookies we use

  • Technical / Necessary Cookies: Essential for the proper functioning of the site (e.g. navigation, mobile menu, cookie banner). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytical Cookies: Used to collect aggregated and anonymous data on how the site is used (Google Analytics). These cookies are loaded only after your explicit consent.

3. How we manage consent

When you first visit the site, a banner appears allowing you to:

  • Click «Accept all» to accept technical and analytical cookies;
  • Click «Manage cookies» to choose whether to enable analytical cookies or not.

Google Analytics is loaded only after you have given consent for analytical cookies. No analytical tracking is activated without your explicit consent.

4. How to modify or withdraw consent

You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the «Manage cookies» link in the banner or in the footer. Your choices will be saved for one year.

Alternatively, you can manage or disable cookies directly through your browser settings.

5. Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services that we use (primarily Google for Analytics and fonts). These cookies are subject to the respective privacy policies of those third parties.
