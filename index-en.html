About Us

Studio Legale Lo Foco stands out for its multidisciplinary approach, high professionalism, and ability to offer innovative solutions in a constantly evolving market. Founded on solid expertise in copyright, administrative, and commercial law, our firm is a point of reference for companies and professionals in the creative and cultural sectors—with a particular focus on the cinema and audiovisual industry. With extensive experience and in-depth knowledge of Italian and European regulations, we support authors, artists, production companies, publishers, and other creative entities in protecting and enhancing their work. Our goal is to provide tailor-made legal consultancy that safeguards our clients’ ideas and strengthens their competitive value.
