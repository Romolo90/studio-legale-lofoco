Avv. Samory Maiga

Copyright and Film & Audiovisual Contracting

Lawyer with specific expertise in copyright law applied to the film and audiovisual industry, with particular focus on entertainment contracting. He provides legal advice and judicial and extrajudicial protection for authors, production companies, and professionals in the cinema, culture, and performing arts sectors.

He combines legal expertise with hands-on experience in the film and audiovisual sector as a director, photographer, and artistic consultant. His profile bridges legal practice with artistic sensitivity and concrete knowledge of creative audiovisual processes.

🎓 Education

  • Master’s Degree in Law – University of Milano-Bicocca
  • Specialization Course for Access to the Legal Profession – Sapienza University of Rome, School of Specialization for Legal Professions

📜 Professional Experience

  • Lawyer at Studio Legale Lo Foco, where he works on copyright law applied to the film and audiovisual industry, with a particular focus on entertainment contracting and the protection of creative works.
  • Trainee Lawyer at Studio Legale Lo Foco, focusing on intellectual property, trademarks, and legal consultancy for the performing arts sector.
  • Hands-on experience as director and photographer in the production of commercials, music videos, and advertising content.
  • Author and director of the documentary “La septième magie”, dedicated to the Cinema Numérique Ambulant (2015-2016).
