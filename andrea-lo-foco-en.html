Attorney Andrea Lo Foco

Expert in Copyright and Administrative Law

An attorney specializing in copyright and administrative law, with particular expertise in the film, audiovisual, and entertainment sectors. He is involved in the editorial and commercial management of rights for the exploitation of audiovisual works, with a focus on cinema tax credit and regulations protecting creative works in Italy and Europe.

A member of the Culture and Entertainment Commission of the Rome Bar Association, he provides legal consultancy for authors, artists, production companies, and publishers, ensuring both contractual and strategic protection in the cultural and creative fields.
