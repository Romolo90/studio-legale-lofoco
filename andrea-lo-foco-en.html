Avv. Andrea Lo Foco

Expert in Copyright and Administrative Law

An attorney with experience in copyright and administrative law, with established activity in the film, audiovisual, and entertainment sectors. He is involved in the editorial and commercial management of rights for the exploitation of audiovisual works, with a particular focus on cinema tax credit and on the protection of creative works in Italy and Europe.

A member of the Culture and Entertainment Commission of the Rome Bar Association, he provides legal consultancy for authors, artists, production companies, and publishers, ensuring both contractual and strategic protection in the cultural and creative fields.
